Why this pattern

Drag-to-create conflicts with scrolling on mobile — the browser can't tell them apart. A default event appears in the first free slot so no creation gesture is needed. Move (drag body or left handle) and resize (44×44 center handles) are distinct gestures with touch-action: none on all interactive targets. Overlapping events split into equal columns automatically.
